2 March 2018

We have been researching delta robots that are capable to draw on a surface. Inspired by the mechanics of 3D delta printers, we believed that the delta structure would work best for our installation. Therefore, we have done research regarding the mechanics of the delta robots and came to a revelation that we require parts like manipulator rods, stepper motors and ball-and-roller links.

Source: Delta bot by mjcbruin, Drawing Delta by Jarkman Industries
